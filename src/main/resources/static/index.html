About Us

Pioneering Smart Grid Security

GridSecure is an innovative IoT-based solution developed to combat the growing challenge of energy theft in power distribution networks. Our system combines cutting-edge hardware with cloud-powered analytics.

Built using ESP8266 microcontrollers, PZEM-004T energy meters, and AWS cloud services, we provide utility companies with an affordable, scalable solution to protect their infrastructure.
